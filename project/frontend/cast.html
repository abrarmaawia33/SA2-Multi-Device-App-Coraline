Henry Selick Jr.

The movie *Coraline* (2009) was written by **Henry Selick**, who also directed the film. However, the screenplay is based on the 2002 novel *Coraline* by **Neil Gaiman**. Selick adapted Gaiman's story into the screenplay, capturing its dark fantasy elements while translating it into an animated film.He is known for his gothic horror films and for directing the stop-motion animated films The Nightmare Before Christmas, James and the Giant Peach, Monkeybone, Coraline, and Wendell & Wild

Characters

Coraline Jones

Coraline Jones is the main character. She is a curious, intelligent, resourceful, and courageous girl.

Wybie Lovat

Wyborn "Wybie" Lovat is Coraline's quirky neighbor and eventual friend. He is instrumental in helping Coraline defeat the Beldam.

Charlie Jones

is Coraline's father. He cares about Coraline very much and is kind, brave, and helpful. He makes "creative" food creations that Coraline strongly dislikes.

Mel Jones

Mel Jones is Coraline's mother. She is very busy most of the time, and sometimes inattentive, but she loves and cares about Coraline.

Cat

is a black cat from the real world, who acts as a mentor to Coraline and guides her through her journey. He is unnamed,

Mr. Bobinsky

Mr. Bobinsky, is a major character of the stop-motion film, Coraline. He is Coraline Jones's Russian neighbor who lives in the upstairs flat of the Pink Palace Apartments.

The Ghost Children

are the spirits of three children, two girls and one boy, who were the previous victims of Beldam.

April Spink & Miriam Forcible

are a pair of retired actresses who live in the flat under Coraline's. They own many aging Scotties and talk in theater jargon, often referencing their glory days as actresses.
